Background

STARGAZER was discovered in 2025 by Tukiso Malatji. It is a new small clothing business launched in 2025, focusing on trendy, eco-friendly apparel for young adults aged 18-35. Based in Polokwane, Stargazer offers unique, celestial-inspired designs like t-shirts, hoodies, and accessories, using sustainable materials like organic cotton. The brand's mission is to provide stylish, inclusive clothing that promotes self-expression and environmental care. Stargazer aims to grow through e-commerce, building a loyal customer base with its ethical and fashionable approach.
